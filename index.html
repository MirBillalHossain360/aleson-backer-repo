Alisson Ramses Becker

(born October 2, 1992)

is a professional football player from Brazil who plays the position of a goalkeeper for Liverpool FC in the English Premier League as well as the Brazillian national team. Currently, the FIFA Best goalkeeper in the world, Alisson Becker’s rise to prominence in the world of sports didn’t start overnight. From the age of 10, Alisson has consistently been training to emerge as one of the best goalkeepers in the world. We take a look at Alisson Becker who is currently making waves in the English Premier League in this edition of biography facts, childhood, career, net worth and personal life.

The enthusiasm and zeal Alisson has to become the best goalkeeper in the world have led him to compete fiercely for the top goalkeeping spot in various clubs where he has played, that competitive spirit saw him defeat his elder brother, also a goalkeeper for the goalkeeping spot while he was at Internacional.

His desire to be number one led him to tussle with retired ace Brazilian goalkeeper, Jesus Silva, popularly known as Dida for the top goalkeeping spot at Internacional. Armed with his youth, swiftness and athleticism, Alisson managed to defeat the star goalkeeper to keep the number one spot, an incredible feat for a youngster.
